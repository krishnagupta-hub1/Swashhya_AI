# **App Name**: Swasthya AI

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Symptom Checker: Allows users to input symptoms and receive a list of potential conditions. Generates possible diagnosis with cross-reference to the AYUSH ministry guidelines, providing preliminary healthcare information. Acts as a tool that considers available health records before responding.
- Personalized Wellness Plans: Generates customized Yoga and Ayurveda-based wellness plans based on user's age, health conditions and location, using local herbs such as Giloy and Ashwagandha, giving access to preventive healthcare.
- Mental Health Support: Analyzes user's text input for sentiment, providing stress-relief tips and mental health support. Serves as a tool that can take recent mood check-ins into account to inform a recommendation.
- Drug Interaction Alerts: Alerts users about potential interactions between prescribed Allopathic and Ayurvedic medicines, promoting responsible and integrated medicine practices.
- Emergency Triage: Detects critical symptoms, based on user input, and redirects to the nearest hospital, providing immediate support during health crises.
- Multilingual Support: Provides support in multiple languages including Hindi, Bengali, and Tamil, ensuring accessibility for diverse users.
- Integration with Government APIs: Connects with government health APIs such as Ayushman Bharat to help patients benefit from existing schemes and get remote healthcare from local PHCs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#75A4FF) to evoke trust and health.
- Background color: Light blue (#EAF0FF), a very desaturated version of the primary color, providing a clean, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft green (#A3E47F) for positive reinforcement and call-to-action buttons.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to ensure legibility and a modern yet approachable feel.
- Use clear and universally recognizable health-related icons.
- Clean, intuitive layout with easy-to-navigate sections.
- Subtle animations for feedback and engagement, e.g., loading indicators.
